Anne Frank House

The Anne Frank House is a museum located in the centre of Amsterdam and contains the hiding Place where Anne Frank hid with her family and wrote her diary during World War II.

Admission is €10

Open daily from 9am -7pm

Van Gogh museum

Van Gogh museum is a showcase of all his paintings and written work. The museum includes his famous self-portrait and scream paintings as well as his friends and apprentice’s art work within the building.

Admission is €20

Open daily from 9am -10pm

The Rijksmuseum

The Rijksmuseum museum shows a collection of art and history of Holland from the middle Ages to current times. The museum highlights the Dutch Golden Age of Rembrandt van Rijn’s Night Watch and Johannes Vermeer’s The Milkmaid which is made visible to the public.

Admission is €21

Open daily from 9am-10pm

A'dam Lookout

A'dam Lookout is the newest attraction to be added in Amsterdam. This the highest tower in Amsterdam which offers an amazing view around the city. It holds the highest swing in Europe on the roof top which is 100 meters above ground nearing the edge of the building.

Admission is €13.50

Open daily from 9am-7pm
