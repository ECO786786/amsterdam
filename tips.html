Get the IAmsterdam card

The IAmsterdam card offers free entrance to a majority of museums and attractions including free transportation around Amsterdam.

Rent a bicycle

Bikes are predominantly used around Amsterdam and are the best way to assimilate in the Dutch culture. You can rent a bike for as little as €10 for the whole day.

Stay in hostels

Hostels are a cheaper alternative to hotels which can be extremely expensive in this city. This can be an amazing opportunity for families to save money traveling to Amsterdam.

Get the Museumkaart Museum Card

The Museumkaart Museum Card allows you to gain accessed to 32 museums in Amsterdam and more than 400 throughout the Netherlands for only €60 which last for a month.

Unpredictable weather

Weather in Amsterdam can be unpredictable. Make sure to pack for all types of weather such as an umbrella for when it rains and coats when temperatures dip.
